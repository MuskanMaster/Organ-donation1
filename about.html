About Us

We are a group of students from SVMIT, Bharuch (Shri S'ad Vidya Mandal Institute of Technology) College, passionate about connecting people through the power of organ donation. Our goal is to spread awareness and build a supportive community around this vital cause.

Our Journey

Our journey started during a college seminar when we first learned about the critical shortage of organ donors. Inspired by the stories of lives saved, we decided to take action by raising awareness among our college community. We began by organizing events and campaigns, reaching out to students and faculty to share the importance of organ donation. What began as a small initiative has now become a growing movement within our college, with plans to expand our outreach further.
